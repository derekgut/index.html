My homework is a program where all of your homework assignments will be laid out on a calender. You can see all your assignments, projects and tests/quizzes will be. If you struggle with organization or are forgetful then this is something you should definitely consider, it is free and is compatible with IOS, Android, Windows, Mac, and Kindle Fire. Go get Myhomework

Evernote is an app that helps you organize all your notes. In online school you will be taking a lot of notes online on your computer. Evernote helps you organize everything you write down, each page of notes can be organized into folders, notebooks, or exported into other app in your computer like Microsoft Word or Google Docs if you need to. You can download the app on your phone so you can finish your notes on the fly if you need to. This app is free and is available on Mac, Windows, IOS, and Android. Go get Evernote

Dropbox is an app that organizes all your documents that you created. Each document you create and name gets placed in a folder you created so everything is organized and wont get lost. Don't worry, your documents wont crash and get deleted thats guaranteed! All you have to do is create an account and upload your documents. This app is free and is available for Mac,Windows,Android,IOS, and Kindle Fire. Go get Dropbox

You have to write a paper and you need a resource page? Easy, the best way to cite your resources is with Easy Bib. Easy Bib lets you put all the information about your article and it creates the citation for you. All you have to do is copy and paste it. Creating your own citations is a waste of time and effort when you can use Easy Bib. You can also upgrade it and can check your paper for any unintentional plagiarism. Easy Bib is something everyone needs for online classes or regular in person school. Its a necessity! Go get Easy Bib

No matter how bad you want to finish your work, facebook, twitter or instagram will always sound better. Self Control helps you with getting your work done. Self Control lets you put any website or app on your computer on lock, you won't be able to get into the app. So if you have a project and need to get it done or need to study for a test this is good for you. You can set what time you will be locked out of the app. If you find yourself getting distracted then this is perfect for you. Go get Self Control

Everyone's life is busy, everyone is always on the go. Any.Do is a great way to help you with this, Any.Do is free and compatible with everything ranging from your mac to your chromebook to your Samsung. Any. Do is one of the greatest free calendars you can have, Any.Do lets you write down anything you have to do. You can write down anything on the calendar and you can write down where that thing is and any other details about that event. I personally use this app on my phone and it help me plan out my days and get things done. I previously talked about writing things down and this is a great way to do that. I really recommend this app. Go download Any.Do here

Even if you go to school, and you have to write a research paper your going to have to look up information about your topic, Mendeley is a great app to use. Mendeley is a way you can organize your information and all the article itself. Before writing your paper I recommend using Mendeley, it can help you organize your ideas to see everything in order instead of all over the place. If your information is more organized then you will be able write better. Go log into Mendeley

If you're taking a world language class and you're having trouble learing and grasping the topic this can help you understand. Duolingo will help you learn whatever language no matter how much you know. It has so many topics you can learn from. It is a great tool to use, it helped me and my friends get better grades in our classes and it will help you too. Go create a Duolingo account

Zoom is probably where most of your classes are going to be, if you download Zoom it will make it a lot easier to join and stay in class. When we used Zoom it took a lot of extra steps to log into the class and got kicked out a couple times. You can just copy and paste the meeting information but it takes a lot of extra work and frustration if doesnt work. The best option is to just download the Zoom app. Go and download Zoom now

If you're are cram studying or trying to better understand a topic in class, whatever class. Studyblue is a good tool to use, studyblue has videos and chapters explaining each topic you might see in every class. Depending on what class you are studying, it even had practice problems, overall Studyblue helped me understand a lot of topics and helped me cram study too. Go to start studying now

Tinycards help you study, if you're not good at studying and feel like you waste time because you get distracted. Tinycards is good for you Tinycards lets you study any topic for any subject. You can study the flash cards they created, or you can play a matching timed game. The game is fun to play and really efficient. I recommend this app, it really helps you with learning new topics. Go download Tinycards

Having it on both lets you have freedom and you don't have to worry about something happening. If your computer isn't working then you can use your phone.
