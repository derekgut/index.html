Evernote is app that helps you organize all your notes. In online school you will be taking a lot of notes online on your computer. Evernote helps you orangize everything you write down, each page of notes can be organized into folders, notebooks, or exported into other app in your computer like Microsoft Word or Google Docs if you need to. You can downlod the app on your phone so you can finish your notes on the fly if you need to. This app is free and is avalible on Mac, Windows, IOS, and Android. https://evernote.com/
