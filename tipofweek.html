  1. A good way to be able to get your work done is to setup a rutine. Get up around the same time and get ready the same way. Then go to class and set up a rutine after class to maximize your time. You dont have to do your school work right after school ends if you dont have time but set a time range to get it done. This will create repetion and will make getting your work done so much easier.
  2. At the end of each week you should refelct on how productive you were. You can do this by writing a couple of notes on your phone or on a piece of paper. Looking back and seeing if you were producitve and getting your work done is important. It shows you if you are getting distracted or arent in a great enviorment for learning.
